About Carpenter

We are passionate about providing high-quality electric saws that meet the needs of professionals and enthusiasts alike.

Founding Sparks: Early 1900s - 1920s

In the early 1900s, Edmund Sawbridge, a visionary with a mechanical mind and a passion for precision, revolutionized the woodworking industry. His first electric saws, crafted with meticulous detail and innovative design, became essential tools for craftsmen, embodying Edmund's dedication to excellence in both performance and durability.

Cutting Edge Today: A Legacy of Precision

Fast forward to the present day, and "Sawbridge" stands as a beacon of cutting-edge technology and reliability. Edmund's legacy endures in every saw, inspiring today's generation of "Sawbridge" enthusiasts to continually innovate and enhance the craft of woodworking.

The Modern "Sawbridge"

Today, "Sawbridge" is more than a brand; it's a thriving community committed to innovation and precision. Our dedication to quality and ingenuity shines through in our diverse range of electric saws, designed to meet the needs of professionals and hobbyists alike.
